Adventure in Ladakh

Biking in Ladakh, Chadar Trek, Jeep Safari, Markha Valley Trek, Snow Leopard Trek, Camping in Ladakh, Camel Safari in Nubra Valley, Quad Biking in Nubra Sand Dunes, Rafting in Zanskar and many more.

Ever dreamt of soaring through the snowy peaks or gliding on frozen rivers? If yes, then Leh Ladakh will make it possible. Pump up your adrenaline rush while participating in the adventure in Ladakh. From rock climbing to paragliding, there is no dearth of adventure in Leh Ladakh.

Apart from the monasteries and Leh bike trips, adventure sports are another gem of this snowy heaven. Each activity is allowed in different months, majorly depending upon the weather. Be it icy cold winds or challenging terrains of the Himalayan range, Leh Ladakh has ample of thrilling options to quench your thirst for adrenaline kicks.
